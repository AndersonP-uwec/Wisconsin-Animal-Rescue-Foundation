    Meet Chester!

    Chester is a yellow and white neutered boy. Our vets estimate he is about 5 years old. He's very vocal and loves attention when he isn't napping. He's not the most active cat but he does enjoy supervised playtime with yarn and all things jingly. He gets along with every animal he meets and is very patient with children. Since he is already neutered, his adoption fee is only $35.

    Meet Roxie!

    Roxie is a brown and white large mixed breed sweetheart. She's not yet spayed and is estimated to be 2 years old. She loves going on walks and running outside so she will need a fair amount of outside time and energy. Roxie's good with other dogs, unsure how she is with cats. Until she's spayed, her adoption fee is $60 plus a $45 deposit that will be refunded once there is proof of her being spayed.

    Meet Lucy!

    Lucy is a playful, spayed grey-brown tabby. She's estimated to be 6 years old but plays like a kitten. She has an allergy to grain so she will need specific food for it that we can recommend. She'd do best in a childfree home as she may scratch when playing unintentionally. Lucy also isn't fond of cats but loves dogs. Her adoption fee is only $35.

    Meet Marsh!

    Marsh is a 3 month old Husky puppy. He will have to be neutered when he is 6-12 months old. As a Husky, he's very vocal. He also is very affectionate and playful. He will need lots of exercise and regular grooming. Marsh gets along with most dogs but is uncertain of cats. He'd do best with homes that have older children as he is untrained and growing large quickly. His adoption fee is $50 plus a deposit of $55 until neutered.

    Meet Soot!

    Soot is a 6 month old chocolate-point Siamese mix. He will need to be neutered soon. He is pretty active for a cat and very food motivated. Portions will need to be monitored for him. He likes a lot of exercise but doesn't care for being outside. Soot is also pretty independent but likes to spend time in the same room as people. He loves playing with other cats so we'd recommend he not be the only cat. His adoption fee is $30 plus a deposit of $30 until neutered.
